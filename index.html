Scrolling text...

We are developing a multi-device platform to improve your journey to work a (link), emphasized text, and strong text.

How?

We are harnessing the data already available on TfL's servers and merging it together to predict the delay to your journey to work. With this data, we will change your alarm to wake you up earlier so you can catch an earlier train.

Why Bother?

Accoring to our research, over 80% of Londoners experience severe delays or worse at least once per week.

When will this be available?

As a team of teenagers, we have entered the SAP Apps for Good competition. If we win, our app could be running by February 2017.

Will this be free?

To a certain extent, yes. We will offer all users one sponsored daily live alarm. To have more than one or to remove adds, we will operate with a freemium model.
